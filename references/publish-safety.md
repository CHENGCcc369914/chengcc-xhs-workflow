# Publish Safety

## Default Rule

Stop before the final publish click.

The user must confirm the final publish action in the current turn.

## Why

- Avoid accidental posting.
- Let the creator check personal and workplace details.
- Let the creator verify visual assets.
- Reduce platform and account risk.

## Pre-Publish Checklist

- Cover exists.
- Title exists and is under or near Xiaohongshu limits.
- Body exists.
- Tags are present but not spammy.
- No private names, screenshots, workplace secrets, or identifying details.
- No medical/mental-health treatment promises.
- No legal, financial, tax, or career guarantee.
- No fake engagement tactics.
- AI is framed as reflection support, not authority.

## Browser Automation Boundary

If browser automation fails:

- Do not repeat blindly.
- Preserve the publish package.
- Tell the user which step is blocked.
- Give one manual fallback action.
