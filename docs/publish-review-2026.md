# 2026 Xiaohongshu Publish Review

This file is a practical review gate, not legal advice.

## Current Baseline

As of 2026-06-08, the review should combine:

1. China's AI generated/synthetic content labeling requirements.
2. Xiaohongshu's 2026 AI governance direction reported by public sources.
3. General Xiaohongshu publishing hygiene for image-text notes.

## Key Rules

### AI Generated / Synthetic Content

If the post uses Image 2 or another AI tool to generate final cards:

- actively mark AI-generated / AI-assisted content if the platform provides that option
- do not remove or falsify AI labels / metadata
- do not make AI-generated content look like a real-life photo, screenshot, person, or event unless it is clearly framed as generated/illustrative

### AI Abuse

Reject:

- AI托管账号
- AI 自动模拟真人互动
- AI 编造个人经历
- fake AI chat screenshots
- fake endorsements or outcomes
- batch-generated low-quality template posts

### Rights

Reject:

- copied creator images
- copied card layouts
- borrowed IP or celebrity likeness
- unauthorized brand/logo use
- unlicensed artwork

### Personal-Growth Claims

Revise:

- mental-health treatment promises
- diagnosis language
- career guarantee
- financial/legal advice
- shame-based or manipulative wording

## Review Decision

Use three outcomes:

- `pass`: ready for WorkBuddy draft handoff
- `revise`: fix listed issues, then rerun review
- `block`: do not publish without creator/legal/platform review
