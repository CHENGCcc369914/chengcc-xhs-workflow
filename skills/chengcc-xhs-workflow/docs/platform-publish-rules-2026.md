## Common Gate For All Platforms

Block or revise if any item fails:

- Target platform is missing.
- AI-generated or AI-assisted final cards, images, videos, voices, virtual people, or synthetic materials are not labeled when the platform requires or exposes a label.
- The post pretends AI-generated material is a real person, real screenshot, real event, or real evidence.
- The story invents personal experience, fake outcomes, fake comments, fake endorsements, fake data, fake chat screenshots, or fake before/after proof.
- The account is framed as AI托管, automated interaction, or batch-generated low-quality content.
- The content copies another creator's title, script, layout, image, IP, character, celebrity likeness, brand logo, font, music, video, or artwork without authorization.
- Screenshots expose private names, avatars, IDs, phone numbers, orders, company/client information, or chat details.
- The copy contains medical, psychological treatment, legal, tax, investment, financial, or career guarantees.
- The copy uses absolute or unverifiable claims such as `一定有效`, `保证`, `全网最低`, `100%`, `彻底治好`, or similar.
- The post uses fear, shame, fake urgency, forced interaction, fake scarcity, or misleading comparison to drive clicks.
- The post includes external traffic diversion, private-domain bait, transaction guidance, or contact methods that are not compliant with the target platform.
- Ads, sponsorships, cooperation, products, services, gifts, lottery, or affiliate content are not clearly disclosed.
- Sensitive categories such as medical, finance, education, law, minors, or public safety appear without qualification or cautious framing.

## Xiaohongshu

Use for `xiaohongshu`.

Required focus:

- Preserve the account posture of real sharing. Avoid fake persona, over-packaged experience, fake data, or fabricated scene evidence.
- If the post uses Image 2 or other AI to generate final cards, use the platform's content type declaration when available, such as AI-generated / AI-assisted content. A light body note can also be used when needed.
- Do not publish fake AI chat screenshots. If a real AI chat screenshot is used, blur private information and frame it as a real conversation excerpt.
- Avoid fake marketing notes, pseudo-experience seeding, exaggerated product or method claims, and low-quality repeated templates.
- Keep title, cover, body, and tags consistent. Body should add value beyond repeating the card text.
- Avoid absolute terms, sensitive-industry claims, unauthorized materials, external transaction diversion, clickbait, competitor disparagement, minors risk, and privacy leakage.

Draft decision hints:

- `pass`: ready for Xiaohongshu Creator Center draft.
- `revise`: fix label, authenticity, privacy, body duplication, or wording risk.
- `block`: fake evidence, unmarked AI synthesis, sensitive claim, illegal content, or unauthorized IP.

## Douyin

Use for `douyin`.

Required focus:

- Apply required short-video / image-text labels when applicable, including AI-generated content labels.
- For AI-generated image-text posts, place the label in the platform label field if available. If not available and disclosure is needed, add a clear note near the title/body ending.
- Do not use AI to create fake news, rumors, scams, vulgar batch content, fake comments, fake interaction, or misleading personas.
- Keep AI generation process evidence when the content is high-risk or could be questioned.
- Do not use false urgency, false scarcity, unprovable lowest-price claims, or misleading live-commerce style wording.
- Avoid unauthorized music, image, video, font, celebrity, brand, or product material.
- Ads,达人合作, products, lottery, benefits, and prices must be clear and not exaggerated.
- Finance content requires qualification and cannot promise returns, recommend specific stocks, or imply代客理财.
- Avoid illegal private-domain diversion or contact-method bait.

Draft decision hints:

- `pass`: labels, rights, claims, and diversion risks are clean.
- `revise`: unclear AI label, exaggerated benefit wording, unclear lottery/product disclosure, or weak source marking.
- `block`: fake information, finance/medical guarantee, illegal diversion, copyright misuse, or unqualified sensitive-industry claim.

## WeChat Channels

Use for `wechat_channels`.

Required focus:

- AI-generated or synthetic content must be actively, clearly, and prominently labeled.
- Apply required short-video labels when applicable, including AI-generated content labels.
- Be extra careful with financial topics. Do not promise investment returns, recommend specific stocks, act as代客理财, or mismatch certification/identity.
- Avoid content that could affect the broader WeChat ecosystem, including WeChat Pay, mini programs, groups, enterprise WeChat, or private-domain chains.
- Avoid external platform diversion, unclear transaction paths, unauthorized materials, and sensitive-industry claims.
- Blur or remove chat records, orders, nicknames, avatars, IDs, and other private information.
- Do not imply official, expert, institution, or authentication endorsement without proof.
- Prices, benefits, refund conditions, restrictions, lottery, and activities must be clear.

Draft decision hints:

- `pass`: labels are prominent and WeChat ecosystem risks are low.
- `revise`: unclear source/AI label, weak privacy masking, vague benefits, or mild diversion risk.
- `block`: finance violation, fake AI/staged content, privacy leak, unauthorized endorsement, or high-risk transaction diversion.

## WeChat Official Account

Use for `wechat_official_account`.

Required focus:

- Do not let AI, scripts, interfaces, or automation replace human creation and publishing. Human substantive involvement must be clear.
- AI can assist drafting, polishing, images, or summarizing, but the article should preserve the creator's real expression and responsibility.
- AI-generated images, videos, source material, fictional/staged content, and re-created content should be clearly and prominently marked where needed.
- Reposts, citations, images, fonts, music, videos, user cases, chat records, review screenshots, and third-party materials need authorization or clear source handling.
- Avoid title-party wording, fearmongering, exaggerated conclusions, false advertising, absolute terms, and performance promises.
- Do not induce sharing, following, clicking, or group forwarding in a non-compliant way.
- Ads, sponsorships, commercial cooperation, gifts, lottery, welfare, forms, mini programs, communities, and private-domain chains need clear rules and privacy handling.
- If personal information is collected through forms, mini programs, groups, or external links, include privacy policy and purpose clarity.

Draft decision hints:

- `pass`: human-authored expression is clear, sources are marked, and privacy/commercial rules are handled.
- `revise`: source marking, AI disclosure, user-case authorization, or inducement wording needs tightening.
- `block`: AI fully replaces human creation/publishing, privacy collection lacks policy, false advertising, or unauthorized copyrighted material.
