## Default IP Role

The default 澄Cc visual character is now the ChengCc healing hand-drawn young
male designer IP, not the old orange fruit mascot and not a generic 3D boy.

- Use the avatar as a small visual anchor, expression sticker, action sticker,
  or scene actor.
- Treat the standard setting image as an identity lock, not as the only usable
  pose. Final cards should create new actions, expressions, outfits, props, and
  scene interactions from the current page topic.
- Do not default to pasting a standing pose or an old generated action into a
  new card. Existing action assets are placeholders unless the user explicitly
  asks to reuse them.
- The primary memory symbols are now the orange `C` necklace, orange/C/orange-fruit
  outfit system, ChengCc/Cc/Ccc marks, and clean healing illustration style.
- The orange phone case remains an optional real-life prop when the scene involves
  phone use, but it is no longer the only required memory symbol.
- Do not turn the person into an orange fruit, animal, unrelated mascot, or
  another creator's character.

## Identity Locks

Always preserve:

- young post-00s Chinese male designer / fresh workplace newcomer
- clean, fun, healing hand-drawn illustration material with light paper grain
- same face, same hair, same head/body proportion
- slightly rounder jaw and chin, never a sharp V-shaped chin
- black natural short hair with a loose 4/6 side part, natural falling fringe,
  layered texture, airy volume
- calm, relaxed, slightly lazy but smart eyes
- subtle orange ring / warm amber tone around the iris-pupil area
- subtle blush and freckles
- exactly three small silver earlobe hoops:
  - left ear: two hoops, both on the earlobe area
  - right ear: one hoop, on the earlobe area
  - no cartilage piercings, no high ear piercings, no extra studs
- orange `C` necklace: black thin cord or fine chain, orange letter-C pendant,
  visible in front-facing and half-side views whenever possible
- outfit must contain at least three of these signals when visible: orange as
  main/accent color, letter C/Cc/Ccc/ChengCc, orange icon, orange slice, orange
  peel grain, orange dots, C-shaped pocket/patch/zipper/line, citrus club style,
  retro loose designer styling
- outfit design should follow the clothing asset reference level: original
  ChengCc clothing assets with orange + C + citrus motifs integrated through
  silhouette, texture, stitching, patch, pocket, drawstring, hat, bag, shoes, or
  pajamas details, not a plain outfit recolor
- optional orange phone case, ChengCc/Cc badge, orange icon, C-shaped moon,
  orange sticker, small plant, blue doodle accent, notebook, laptop, design draft

## Hard Avoids

- Do not redesign the character.
- Do not change face proportions, hair structure, earring count/placement, or
  eye orange ring.
- Do not remove or replace the orange `C` necklace in visible front/half-side
  views. Never replace it with a cross, brand logo, skull, or unrelated symbol.
- Do not freeze the character into one repeated pose. The identity stays fixed;
  the action, expression, gesture, outfit, and mini-scene should change according
  to the page meaning.
- Do not make the outfit a plain orange T-shirt with no design system. The outfit
  needs orange + C + orange-fruit/citrus detail + retro loose designer feeling.
- Do not use ordinary basic T-shirts, designless solid-color clothes, random
  streetwear logos, real brand marks, dark punk/skull elements, overly childish
  or feminine styling, dirty/old textures, or plastic AI-looking materials.
- Do not add real brand logos, celebrity likeness, borrowed IP, extra people, or
  multi-character mascot groups.
- Do not make the character Q-version low-childish, plastic doll, anime boy,
  orange fruit, animal, hyperreal human, cold AI assistant, female-coded, or
  oil-smooth 3D avatar.
- Do not let the character compete with the card headline unless the user asks
  for a character-first sticker sheet.
