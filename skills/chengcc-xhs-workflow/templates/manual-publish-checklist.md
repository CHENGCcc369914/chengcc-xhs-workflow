# Manual Publish Checklist

## Goal

Prepare a human-readable publishing checklist. The creator uploads images, pastes copy, checks labels, and clicks publish manually.

## Target Platform

- Platform code: xiaohongshu / douyin / wechat_channels / wechat_official_account
- Publish surface:
- Creator account:
- Review result: pass / revise / block

If review is `revise` or `block`, do not proceed until the creator approves the fix.

## Manual Operation

Use the matching publish route:

- `xiaohongshu`: Xiaohongshu Creator Center image-text publishing page.
- `douyin`: Douyin Creator Service / publishing page for image-text or video.
- `wechat_channels`: WeChat Channels Assistant / publishing page.
- `wechat_official_account`: WeChat Official Account editor.

Then the creator manually:

- Uploads or places images in the order below.
- Pastes title, body/caption/article text, tags/topics, and any required topic fields.
- Marks AI-generated / AI-assisted content according to platform options when final cards were generated by Image 2 or other AI tools.
- If no AI label field is visible but the review requires disclosure, adds this light disclosure to the body/caption: `图片由 AI 辅助生成，内容来自我的真实经历整理。`
- Adds source/fiction/staged/commercial/activity labels when the review says they are needed.
- Runs the final human check before clicking publish.

## Final Human Check

- [ ] Image order is correct.
- [ ] Cover is the first image.
- [ ] Title is pasted correctly.
- [ ] Body/caption is pasted correctly.
- [ ] Tags/topics are relevant and not spammy.
- [ ] Private names, avatars, IDs, company/client info, and screenshots are masked.
- [ ] AI label/disclosure is handled according to review result.
- [ ] No accidental external link, fake claim, or sensitive detail remains.

The creator decides whether to click publish manually.
